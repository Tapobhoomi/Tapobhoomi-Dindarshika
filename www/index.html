About Sadguru

INTRODUCTION

India is distinguished as the land of Saints and Sages with Spiritualism ingrained in its core beliefs, rich heritage and diverse culture. 'Tapobhoomi', is a spiritually celebrated, internationally renowned place, which locates itself in the heart of Kundaim, Ponda Goa.It has an awe- inspiring achievements to its credit, attained under the diligently foresighted guidance of the present Peethadhishwar of 'PADMANABH SHISHYA SAMPRADAYA', the Ambassador of Peace, H.H DharamabhushanSadguruBrahmeshanandacharya Swami Maharaj. This Sampradaya upholds the divine lineage and tradition of the Guru-ShishyaParampara.

EARLY LIFE

His grace, PujyaSadguru, ascended the throne of the holy 'GURUPEETH', at the tender age of twenty one, a truly noble son of the Goan soil. His holiness, PujyaSwamiji strenuously strives towards reviving the lost historical identity of Goa as 'Deva Bhoomi' and present an authentic picture of Goa in the Worldmap.His positively energizing stature and charisma attracts humongous followers, and the gathering keeps growing substantially.His guidance has proved to be a unfathomable spiritual metamorphosis for numerous. A treasured gem, a golden chalice overflowing with the wisdom of Vedic Science and Dharamagyan.A fountain of rejuvenating positivity, the reason why inquisitive yet highly enlightened minds cherish the moments spent with his grace. His charisma draws together minds from across geographical, ethnic, religious, political, philosophical and other rigid boundaries and also works towards providing a refined, spiritual living to the people from poor, lower, economically stranded strata of society.

SPIRITUAL EXPERIENCE

The GURU-SHISHYA doctrine is meticulously observed by all followers of the P.P.Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday. The flame of ‘SADGURUTATWA’ is kept close to the soul of all disciples of the Sadguru.A quest for the power called GOD is simplified for the shishyas thro verbal elaborations or the ASHIRWACHANS of the Sadguru for Self-realization.Each soul is connected to the superpower thro BHAKTI. The real genuine search of the lord almighty ending in love. Remembrance of the guru thro the DHYAN or meditation, flowing like an unbroken stream of oil poured from one vessel to another. This kind of remembering has been taught in relation to GOD in the PadmanabhShishyaSampraday .THE CONSTANT REMEMBERING IS A MEANS TO LIBERATION . The relentless efforts of Sadgurumaulito Revive the lost, Uphold the lineageof the Saints, Enlighten the living, Instill Universal Brotherhood, Infuse harmony and uplift humanity above all, has made Goa, a destination of Enlightened Spirituality and has given Goa a truer and more authentic picture of itself.One Darshan and Vichar of his grace transforms the yin into yang, egotistic into compassionate, ultimately one humbly bows down to the charisma of the 'Lotus Feet' of this holy 'Gurupeeth' to quench his thirst of self-realization.The 'Gurupeeth' is the sole delivering entity of 'SatyaShreshtaIshwariDnyan' i.e 'Supreme knowledge of truth'. The doctrine of the SATYA SHRESHTHA ESHWARI DNYANA or the divine supreme truth of knowledge devoid of discrimination on the basis of caste or creed , devoid of blind faith or prejudice is the main mantra of this Sampraday, which has made itself eligible to work in collaboration for the World famous Peace Conferences from time immemorial.

THE GREAT INDIAN TRADITION

India is a land of saints’ seer’s philosophers and pious religious leaders. They have gifted humanity the doctrine of peace unity and brotherhood through their work and through the ideal lives they lived. The culturally religious BHARATBHOOMI is also known as the DHARMABHOOMI as we Indians obey and observe all our socio-religious obligations steadfastly reference in the BHAGWAD GITA mentions that god almighty incarnated himself every time there was a breach in these religiousness and righteousness so as to reset this spiritual fabric of truth and dharma. Many such incarnations throughout the ancient times have been witnessed amongst us specially of the three headed god LORD DATTATREYA who is a combined trident power of the BRAHMA VISHNU and MAHESHA. These incarnations have taken physical form in the form of guru or spiritual masters as in the PARAMPUJYA PADMANABH SHISHYA SAMPRADAY. In times of fanatism, nepotism, corruption addiction adversities and immorality this positive energy has sprung from the universe as shriDattamaharaj…shripadvallabh..ShriNarsimhasaraswati..shri Akkalkotswamiji…shriswamisamarth..Shrisiddapadacharya swami maharaj…shripadmanabhacharya swami maharaj..shrisusenacharyaswamimaharaj..shrisadanandacharya swami maharaj..shriBrahmanandacharya swami maharaj and the present pithadishwar of the padmanabhshishyasampraday H.H .DharmabhushanshrimadsadguruBrahmeshanandacharya swami maharajji.

The women are empowered to build a colorful social fabric thro the motherly contributions of JagatJanani.The HINDU sanathan Dharma should blaze and soar high thro the Dharma Shastras. All work is put up on a large canvass of 76 Santsamaj or regional organizations throughout Goa by efficient administration, unitedly and ethically by the Santsamaj Sanghatana and governing all the above is the Pradhan Mandal, the governing body after the Supreme hierarchy of SADGURUDEV.

About Sampraday
India / Bharat is a sacred land having a rich cultural heritage. This land has borne great many saints and religious leaders who have set a universal bench mark in their transcendental knowledge and experiences. Each one has set a history of spiritual heritage through their exemplary lives. Through these saints the whole of mankind has reached close to Godliness.

All Hindu psychics prophets, philosophers, Sadhus and Saints have dwelt on the one Vedic concept put forth by our ancient Rishis, Munis and that is the doctrine of self realization and Moksha. The teaching of the Sapta Rishis has continue to live in the lives of the Hindus through the relentless efforts of the Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday ancestry which started from the “MAHAESHA”, the Sri Guru Naradacharya, Sri Dattaprabhu, Shripad Vallabh Swami, Sri Narsinh Saraswati Swami, Sri Akkalkot Swami Samartha, Sri Siddhapadacharya Swami, Sri Padmanabhacharya Swami, Sri Sadanandacharya Swami, Sri Brahmanandacharya Swami, to the present Peethadeshwar Dharmabhushan Sadguru Brahmeshanandacharya Swami Maharaj. The Sampradayas 1st contributions to self and society was commenced by the reverend Padmanabhacharya Swamiji so has taken the name after its pioneer as “Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday”. It seems Dutta Prabhu bifurcated the superpower into Sripad vallabh and Sri Narsinha Saraswati. After finishing his divine work on mother Earth one of the incarnations i.e. Swami Narsinha Saraswati disappeared into thickets of Kardali forest near Shailya Parvat.

The divine work included a threefold aspect of knowledge, devotion and good deeds so as to establish the satya sanatan vaidik dharma to tread the path of righteousness and attain self realization. Later during the British era, most people were misleaded by greed and selfishness and faltered in the path of their dharma. This was the time the Superpower reappeared as Swami Samarth to guide his people who had gone astray and continue the divine work of the Mahaesha through the Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday. This Sampraday believes in “Guruseva” the core base of its doctrine and teachings.

The Hindu Sanatan Sanskriti marches forward through the Hindu Dharma Pracharaks (Teachers) who spread Hindu Shastras and awareness in society. Thus prevent getting influenced by other religious concepts ! of which we are proud of. This bonding of Sanatan dharma is exhibited in the form of Bhakti Mahotsav, Bhakti Kumbhotsav, Anusthan Saptah, Bhagwad Saptah, Divya Devasthan sabha, Sanskrit Mahasammelans, Atharvashirsha Pathan Mahasohala, Sadguru Janmotsav and many many more … the doctrine of self realization helps in uplifting masses, the mass prayer sessions develop positive energy fields in the environment thus helping every individual travel to the highest peak of total consciousness irrespective of gender, caste, creed, class or race. Infinite followers of the Padmanabh Sampraday continue to cherish the marvelous and miraculous experiences of the supreme master the Sadguru.

It is this devotion, Principles and philanthropic approach of each individual of Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday that has its contribution globally and would continue still for generations hereafter…
