About Swamiji
“Tapobhoomi”… Goa’s renowned spiritual hub located at Kundaim in Ponda Taluka has reached great heights due to the incessant efforts of the present Pithadeeshwar of the renowned “PADMANABH SHISHYA SAMPRADAY” his holiness (H.H.) Dharmabhushan Swami Brahmeshanandacharya Maharaj. The revered Swamiji has been instrumental in reviving Goa’s lost historical identity as “DEVBHUMI” and not just a hub for tourism oriented fun and frolic.

This paradise has been gifted with a bounty of natural resources sun, sand and sea, along with powerhouses of positive energy the Goan temples. The native of this land are god fearing people and tolerant with a “LIVE AND LET LIVE” attitude. Thus sowing the spiritual seeds of the Padmanabh Sampraday was spiritually conducive here but times were turbulent and tyrannical amidst religious turmoil… yet this Sampraday (Cult) sprang its roots firmly with “SADGURUS” devine intervention for the “VEDIC SANATAN DHARMA”. This Dharma advocates tolerance which is the alfa and omega to live a fruitful life to mankind. The mantras chants, Vedic techniques of worship and meditation taught to crude minds and bodies made them peaceful and optimistic even in turbulent times. This spiritual meta morphosis has been achieved from ages by the Goan layman by divine togetherness and company of the “SADGURU”, thereby helping in Social, Ecological and Political transformation for Goa’s dawn of liberation and freedom.

This noble ancestry of Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday dates back to H.H. Shri Sushenacharya Swami Maharaj, H.H. Shri Sadanandacharya Swami Maharaj, H.H. Shri Brahmanandacharya Swami Maharaj to the present heir to this spiritual throne H.H. Dharmabhushan Shri Brahmeshanandacharya Swami Maharaj the reverend Swamiji ascended the “GURUPITH” throne at a very young and tender age of 21 years. A noble son of Goan Soil. Swamiji is a rare and precious royal state treasurer. A golden chalice overflowing with the Vedic Science and dharmadnyana. A fountain of rejuvenating positivity that is the reason why not just blank and inquisitive minds but global leaders both Religious and political along with great seers, godmen, saint and philosophers worldwide flock to this jewel… the “Dharmabhushan”….

His relentless efforts for a decade and half has put Goa on the global map in fields of various contributions for peace to the human race. One darshana and vichar of P. Swamiji converts the bad into good… the yin into Yan and each one bows in these lotus feet to quench the thirst of self- realization thro this “ Charisma” of this unique “Gurupeeth” which is the sole patent of the “ SATYA SHRESTHA ISHWARI DNYAN”… the supreme knowledge of truth.
About Sampraday
India / Bharat is a sacred land having a rich cultural heritage. This land has borne great many saints and religious leaders who have set a universal bench mark in their transcendental knowledge and experiences. Each one has set a history of spiritual heritage through their exemplary lives. Through these saints the whole of mankind has reached close to Godliness.

All Hindu psychics prophets, philosophers, Sadhus and Saints have dwelt on the one Vedic concept put forth by our ancient Rishis, Munis and that is the doctrine of self realization and Moksha. The teaching of the Sapta Rishis has continue to live in the lives of the Hindus through the relentless efforts of the Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday ancestry which started from the “MAHAESHA”, the Sri Guru Naradacharya, Sri Dattaprabhu, Shripad Vallabh Swami, Sri Narsinh Saraswati Swami, Sri Akkalkot Swami Samartha, Sri Siddhapadacharya Swami, Sri Padmanabhacharya Swami, Sri Sadanandacharya Swami, Sri Brahmanandacharya Swami, to the present Peethadeshwar Dharmabhushan Sadguru Brahmeshanandacharya Swami Maharaj. The Sampradayas 1st contributions to self and society was commenced by the reverend Padmanabhacharya Swamiji so has taken the name after its pioneer as “Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday”. It seems Dutta Prabhu bifurcated the superpower into Sripad vallabh and Sri Narsinha Saraswati. After finishing his divine work on mother Earth one of the incarnations i.e. Swami Narsinha Saraswati disappeared into thickets of Kardali forest near Shailya Parvat.

The divine work included a threefold aspect of knowledge, devotion and good deeds so as to establish the satya sanatan vaidik dharma to tread the path of righteousness and attain self realization. Later during the British era, most people were misleaded by greed and selfishness and faltered in the path of their dharma. This was the time the Superpower reappeared as Swami Samarth to guide his people who had gone astray and continue the divine work of the Mahaesha through the Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday. This Sampraday believes in “Guruseva” the core base of its doctrine and teachings.

The Hindu Sanatan Sanskriti marches forward through the Hindu Dharma Pracharaks (Teachers) who spread Hindu Shastras and awareness in society. Thus prevent getting influenced by other religious concepts ! of which we are proud of. This bonding of Sanatan dharma is exhibited in the form of Bhakti Mahotsav, Bhakti Kumbhotsav, Anusthan Saptah, Bhagwad Saptah, Divya Devasthan sabha, Sanskrit Mahasammelans, Atharvashirsha Pathan Mahasohala, Sadguru Janmotsav and many many more … the doctrine of self realization helps in uplifting masses, the mass prayer sessions develop positive energy fields in the environment thus helping every individual travel to the highest peak of total consciousness irrespective of gender, caste, creed, class or race. Infinite followers of the Padmanabh Sampraday continue to cherish the marvelous and miraculous experiences of the supreme master the Sadguru.

It is this devotion, Principles and philanthropic approach of each individual of Padmanabh Shishya Sampraday that has its contribution globally and would continue still for generations hereafter…
